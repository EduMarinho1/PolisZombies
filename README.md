# PolisZombies

During my Bachelor's, I had a personal goal, finish a videogame by myself. Therefore, on my free-times for those 4 years, I have developed a game called PolisZombies, a 2D top-down shooter inspired on Black Ops Zombies. The player needs to survive through 21 waves in order to win the game. As a Computer Science student, art is not my strength, so I focused my effort on delivering more in-depth gameplay mechanics and variety to bring the best potential for the game.

To play it, download the PolisZombiesGameBuild.zip file, than extract the file, and click on the PolisZombies exe file.
To exit the game, simply press "esc".

The scripts I wrote are on PolisZombiesDevelopment/Assets/Resources/Scripts.

# Gameplay

Survive against relentless waves of zombies as the difficulty increases over time. Each wave introduces new challenges, including a variety of enemies and powerfull boss encounters.

Customize your playstyle by getting your preferred weapons, perks, and weapon attachments to improve your chances of survival.
Explore the map carefully. Hidden are secrets, easter eggs, and multiple endings awaiting to be discovered.

Stay alert, plan your strategy, and see if you can make it through all 21 waves!
